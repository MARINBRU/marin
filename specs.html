🔋 Battery Details

  • Cells: 240× Samsung 40T (P42A) 21700
  • Config: 20s12p (72V nominal, 50Ah)
  • Continuous: 540A (45A per cell × 12 parallel)
  • Burst: 700-900A
  • Layout: Two 10s12p blocks, series-connected with copper bar
  • BMS: 20s programmable with Bluetooth (JBD or ANT)
  • Supplier: EM3ev (custom order)

⚙️ Motor & Controller

  • Motor: QS Motor 138 70H (10-12kW) or 90H (15-18kW)
  • Controller: Votol EM-200 — FOC sine wave, 60-96V, 200A cont / 500A peak
  • Gearing: 12-14T front sprocket, 48-60T rear (~4:1 to 5:1 ratio)
  • Chain: 520 or 525 heavy-duty

📐 Frame & Chassis Plan

  • Approach: Donor frame conversion (prove powertrain first, custom frame for v2)
  • Target donor: Honda Grom / Kawasaki Z125 / small dirt bike ($500-1,500 used)
  • Suspension: From donor or salvaged sportbike front end
  • Brakes: Hydraulic disc, braided stainless lines

📊 Performance Targets

  • Top speed: 70-90 mph (depending on gearing)
  • 0-60 mph: ~4-5 seconds
  • City range: 60-80 miles
  • Highway range (60mph): 40-50 miles
  • Weight: ~120-140 kg (265-310 lb)

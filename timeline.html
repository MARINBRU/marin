📅 Project Timeline

Target: first ride in 3-5 months (solo builder, evenings/weekends).

Jun 18, 2026

✅ Project Launched

Business plan ratified, site launched, specs finalized. Battery architecture locked: 20s12p P42A.

Week 1 (Jun 18-24)

📋 Phase 0: Source & Order

Find donor bike on FB Marketplace. Order QS 138 motor + Votol EM-200. Send battery dimensions to EM3ev. Register domain, deploy site.

Week 2-3

🔧 Phase 1: Donor Prep

Strip donor bike. Remove engine, fuel tank, exhaust. Clean frame. Measure battery cavity. Send final dimensions to EM3ev. Order remaining parts (contactor, fuse, DC-DC, lights).

Week 3-4

🔩 Phase 2: Motor & Controller Mount

Fabricate motor mount bracket (steel plate, welded or bolted). Mount QS 138. Align chain/sprockets. Mount controller in airflow. Bench-test motor + controller with low-voltage PSU.

Week 4-5

🔋 Phase 3: Battery Arrives & HV Wiring

EM3ev pack arrives. Wire main fuse (400A class T), main contactor, precharge circuit. Route HV cables (35-50mm²) to controller. Double-check polarity. First power-on (no load).

Week 5-6

💡 Phase 4: Low-Voltage System

Install DC-DC converter. Wire 12V distribution. Lights (headlight, taillight, turn signals). Dash/speedometer. Horn, brake light switch. Handlebar switchgear.

Week 6-7

🏁 Phase 5: First Power & Tune

Full system checkout. Controller programming (conservative settings first). Test throttle response. Check for overheating, loose connections. Short test ride.

Week 7-10

🔁 Phase 6: Road Test & Iterate

Suspension setup (sag, damping). Controller tuning (phase current, field weakening). Range test. Top speed run. Fix issues. V2 improvements.

🎯 Current Status

Phase 0: Planning & Sourcing

Next milestone: Donor bike acquired and parts ordered.
